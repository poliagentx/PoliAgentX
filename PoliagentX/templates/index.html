Welcome to poliagent x

PPI is an artificial intelligence framework that
models the relationship between government spending
and development outcomes in a multidimensional and
highly interconnected environment.



To use the app, you need to go through five steps.
Each step provides instructions and a spreadsheet
template that you should use to prepare your data.
At the end of the workflow, you will be able to visualise
your simulation outputs and download the relevant data.

steps to follow

Step 1: indicators
you should collect time series of indicators
across different policy dimensions (e.g.,
education,health, pollution).


Step 2: Budget
This is information about the total
government expenditure


Step 3: Network
This is information about the various dependencies
that exist in the government bodies.


Step 4: Calibration
This is where model calibration occurs


Step 5: Simulation
This is running the different scenarios that
give one the results.
