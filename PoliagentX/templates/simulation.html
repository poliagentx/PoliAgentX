Simulation Parameters

PoliagentX always divides the time span into 50 'simulation periods' Where these simulation periods give the agents inside the model time to learn. If your data spans 10 years, 1 simulation period of the simulation will be equal to 10 ÷ 50 = 2.4 months So you use that figure to tell the model how many future simulation periods it should simulate Hence if you want to simulate 5 years into the future you would specify 25 simulation periods
