A tool aimed to provide policymakers with information to effectively participate in evidence-based policy advocacy, resulting in efficient government resource allocation and better development outcomes.

About PoliAgentX

PoliAgentX is a data-driven policy planning platform tailored for Kenya. Inspired by the global Policy Priority Inference (PPI) tool, we empower governments to identify real policy priorities, optimize public spending, and design smarter development strategies.

What problem are we trying to solve?

In many governments, there is a disconnect between stated goals and actual spending. Resources are limited, and decisions are often influenced by political or institutional constraints rather than data. This results in inefficiencies, unaddressed development gaps, and missed opportunities for progress.

How do we plan to solve it?

PoliAgentX uses policy simulations, expenditure data, and governance modeling to reveal what priorities are really being implemented. It helps decision-makers test new policy mixes and align resources with the areas that need them most. Our goal is to support a smarter, more transparent, and impact-focused public sector in Kenya.

What is our website about?

This site introduces PoliAgentX, showcases how it works, and demonstrates how it can help Kenya improve policy decisions. You'll find insights, project details, and ways to get involved or learn more. We're here to bring evidence-based planning to the heart of government action.
